--- title: Quickly inserting images from desktop author: '' date: '2018-06-29' tags: - Tutorial slug: quickly-inserting-images-from-desktop ---

Suppose you took a screen cap on a mac using Shift-Command-4. You can use my RStudio addin “Insert image from desktop” to automatically add the image to the post. In general, the addin copies the newest .jpg, .png., or .gif file into the blogdown website directory.
